Wizards and Guilds

Welcome to the Mystic Circle my liege. Our wizards are awaiting your commands. Utilizing the runes obtained from our towers, they can cast a variety of spells to enrich our province or turn their mystic powers against our enemies. They will be able to cast as many spells as we wish while they have sufficient mana and the runes required for each spell. Unfortunately, if their mana drops below 10% they will need to rest, but fear not as they will regain mana each day. Based on our race and personality we will have access to special spells.

Self Spells

Our wizards have an impressive range of spells they can use to enhance and protect oour province. Some spells will remain on our province for a period of time while others will remain in place until triggered. The duration of our self spells is based on the percentage of acres dedicated to guilds.

Here is a brief summary on the spells available to all provinces within Utopia:

  • Minor Protection - Increases our physical defenses
  • Magic Shield - Increases our magical defenses
  • Fertile Lands - Increase food production
  • Nature's Blessing - A multi-purpose spell that has a chance to instantly cure Plague per cast and also protects our land from Storms and Droughts for its duration.
  • Love & Peace - Increase birth rate of peasants
  • Builders Boon - Decreases construction time
  • Patriotism - Increases the rate at which soldiers are drafted
  • Paradise - Rogue, Mystic (Not available to Faery) - An expensive spell to instantly grant you a small amount of land from our kingdom's exploration pool, however this spell is not available during fortified stance or War
  • Anonymity - This spell remains until activated. Activate this spell by attacking, we hide in the shadows while we attack our enemy, our province name will not be displayed in the news; however, we will be penalized with reduced combat gains and no honor.
  • Shadowlight - This spell remains until activated. It guarantees to identify the province that performs the next successful thievery operation against our land.

The exclusive spells available to certain races and personalities are:

  • Greater Protection - Avian, Humans, Faeries, Clerics - Increases our physical defenses, this spell can be cast in addition to Minor Protection.
  • Tree of Gold - Faeries - Money doesn't grow on trees, or does it? With this spell you will instantly generate a portion of your daily income
  • Invisibility - Faeries - Increases the strength of our thieves
  • Clear Sight - Elf, Faery, Tacticians - Provides a chance to catch enemy thieves
  • Fanaticism - Avians, Faeries, Warrior - Invokes a berserker state of mind in our military to increase offensive abilities at the cost of defenses
  • Mage's Fury - Faeries - Enrages our wizards to increase their offensive abilities at the cost of magical defenses
  • Revelation - Faery, Heretics, Mystics, Rogues - Increases the chance of recruiting a new scientist each day.
  • Town Watch - Faeries - Uses peasants to join our defenses to slightly bolster them.
  • Bloodlust - Avians, Orcs, Faeries - Insights rage within our troops to increase their offensive abilities and kill more enemy troops at a cost of higher losses in our army.
  • Aggression - Dryad, Faery - Provokes our soldiers to slightly increase their offense at the cost of some defense.
  • Reflect Magic - Orcs, Faeries - Provides a protective magical barrier that provides a chance to reflect offensive spells back to the caster.
  • Quick Feet - Humans, Faeries - This spell remains until activated. Increase the speed of our next military strike so they return faster.
  • Mystic Aura - Dwarves, Faeries - This spell remains until activated. Guaranteed to repel the next offensive spell cast against you.
  • Animate Dead - Faery, Cleric - This spell remains until activated. After our next defensive battle, half of our dead troops will be revived as soldiers.
  • War Spoils - Faery, War Hero - We revel in our victory and the land from any attack is immediately added to our province.

Paladin Spells

The Paladin's divine spirit yields supreme power in supporting their Allies. With this power comes unique spells that are only available to the Paladin derived from the mastery of their skills. These spells provide immense benefits that a Paladin cannot fathom selfishly keeping to themselves. They must use their mana wisely to effectively cover their Allies with spells that will prove most beneficial.

Here is a brief summary on the spells available exclusively to Paladins:

  • Paladin's Inspiration - Your nobility as Paladin inspires courage and passion with the conscripts allowing significantly faster train time and lower wages for the duration.
  • Scientific Insights - Experience gained from others can prove to be the difference in a battle won or lost, with this spell your Paladin provides his invaluable scientific experience to enhance your scientists knowledge of the arts.
  • Illuminate Shadows - Your Paladin is ever vigilant, providing a watchful eye over the lands and reducing the effectiveness of your enemies' thievery operations
  • Wrathful Smite - The battle prowess of a Paladin is unmatched and with this spell provides your military exceptional training in afflicting the maximum amount of damage to your enemies
  • Divine Shield - The Paladin grants the recipient of this spell a token of his Divine power providing a boost to defensive magical capabilities.

Sorcery

Our wizards can also assist in attacking and weakening our enemies. They can cast spells that will cause damage over several days and others that will cause immediate damage. Due to the aggressive nature of some spells, they may only be cast on our enemies that have a more combative relationship to our kingdom. The success and duration of our offensive spells depends on how many wizards we have per acre, the enemy's wizards per acre, and the size difference between the networth of our provinces.

The spells that may be cast by any province against our enemies include:

  • Storms - Invoke the power of mother nature to kill a small portion of our enemies peasants each day. Cancels the drought impacting the same province.
  • Droughts - Call upon mother nature to decrease their food production, military draft rate and horse productions each day. Clears any storms that ravage the same province.
  • Expose Thieves - Illuminates the enemy's land to reduce the available stealth of their thieves.
  • Greed - Encourages enemy's military to demand additional wages while active.
  • Explosions - Mines the trade routes of the province with explosives that have a chance to destroy a portion of any aid sent to or from the target province

The exclusive spells available to certain races or personalities include:

  • Gluttony - Faery, Rogue - Persuade the enemy population to consume more food
  • Pitfalls - Faeries, War Heroes - Place magical traps on target province that increases military casualties

The aggressive spells that require an unfriendly relationship between our kingdom and the target's kingdom include:

  • Fireball - Calls down a large fireball to destroy a small portion of the enemy's peasants
  • Lightning Strike - Direct lightning towards the enemy's towers to destroy a portion of the enemy's runes
  • Mystic Vortex - Summons a mystical vortex that has a chance to remove each active spell on the target province
  • Tornadoes - Destroys a small portion of the enemy's buildings
  • Land Lust - An expensive and difficult spell that removes our enemy's land and awards it to us

The specialized spells that certain races or personalities can cast against the province's of an unfriendly kingdom are:

  • Fool's Gold - Faeries, Paladin - Casts a reverse alchemy spell on the enemy to turn a portion of their gold into useless lead
  • Amnesia - Faery, Sage - Create memory loss in enemy scientists causing them to forget some of their experience which may result in some reverting to a lower level
  • Nightmares - Faery, Heretic - Disrupts the sleep of a portion of the military causing trained troops to become soldiers while soldiers will simply quit and return to peasantry
  • Meteor Showers - Faery, Mystics - A devastating duration spell that will kill a portion of peasants and troops at home each day
  • Chastity - Faery, Mystics - Coax the enemy's population to stop reproducing and cease all peasant births
  • Blizzard - Faery, Heretic - A short duration spell that will reduce building efficiency
  • Magic Ward - Paladin - The Paladin influences an enemies' wizards to require additional runes to cast spells
  • Barrier of Integrity - Paladin - A very short duration spell that instills fear amongst your enemy, ceasing all soldier drafting that would take place
