Using your Thieves

Moving through the shadows, our thieves are an essential part to our kingdom operations to obtain information about our enemies and disrupt their operations. The success rate of our operations depends on our thievery effectiveness relative to that of our enemy. Thievery effectiveness is primarily determined by the number of thieves we have in relation to the size of our lands. Factors that contribute to our overall thievery effectiveness may include racial and personality bonuses, our honour level, our investment in Crime science, and any Thieves’ Dens we have built. However – no matter how effective our thieves, they can also be thwarted by our enemies’ Watch Towers, or the Clear Sight spell. Our thieves rely heavily on their ability to remain stealthy and slip in and out of the shadows, as such they will not perform any actions once their stealth drops below 10%.

Thief Operations

Gathering intelligence on your enemies is a crucial part of warfare and our thieves have a variety of different operations to obtain this information. To ensure accurate results, you should send 10% of your thieves on each infiltration

The reconnaissance missions available are:

  • Spy on Throne - Provides an overall insight on the resources and troops of a province.
  • Spy on Military - Provides details on enemy troop details, such as troops at home and those out on missions as well as any training occurring.
  • Survey - Display the completed buildings in the enemy province.
  • Spy on Science - Infiltrate the scientists' universities to provide details on how advanced their studies are.
  • Infiltrate - Penetrate the enemy's land to obtain how many thieves they have trained.
  • Snatch News - Sneak into the enemy's land and read their news from the beginning of the previous month.

A diverse range of offensive thievery operations is available to damage our enemies, and the rogue personality offers specialized training for additional attacks as well. Due to the aggressive nature of some operations, a minimum relationship to the enemy's kingdom is required. Learn about The Hostile Meter for more information regarding the relationships between Kingdoms.

The sabotage operations available to all provinces are:

  • Rob the Granaries - Steal a generous amount of food.
  • Rob the Vaults - Steal a small portion of gold from the enemy's coffers.
  • Rob the Towers - Steal a moderate amount of runes from your enemy's towers.
  • Kidnapping - Abduct a very small portion of the enemy's peasants and returns some of these to our province.
  • Sabotage Wizards - Exhaust enemy wizards to lower their available mana.
  • Bribe Thieves - Reduce the enemy's thievery effectiveness for a time until they find the double agents.
  • Bribe Generals - Provides a chance for the enemy's military to incur greater losses until the turncoat is discovered and executed.
  • Arson - Burns down a small amount of enemy buildings, reverting the acres to barren lands.
  • Incite Riots - Disrupt the tax collections for several days which decreases the enemy's income.
  • Steal War Horses - Captures a moderate amount of the enemy's horses still at home, half are released and half become available for our use.
  • Free Prisoners - Infiltrate the opponent's dungeons and release a portion of the prisoners.
  • Night Strike - Requires a minimum relation of Unfriendly - Assassinates a portion of the enemy's military, regardless of whether they are at home or away on a mission.

Additionally, Rogues can access a number of powerful exclusive operations, these are:

  • Greater Arson - Requires a minimum relation of Unfriendly – Similar to Arson, but allows thieves to target a specific type of building. A portion of the selected buiding type is burnt and reverted to barren acres.
  • Assassinate Wizards - Requires a minimum relation of Unfriendly - A difficult operation that will slaughter a very small portion of the enemy's wizards.
  • Propaganda - Only available during War - Attempts to convince enemy soldiers, elites, specialists, thieves or wizards to revolt and join our ranks.
